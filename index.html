TRONS - MISSION BRIEFING

OBJECTIVE:
Fight criminal forces through the city and unlock your technological powers! Reach level 100 to complete the ultimate justice mission and ensure Fair Play.

BASIC CONTROLS:
• Arrow Keys - Steer Terrific Quest high-tech vehicle
• SPACE - DISCO jump
• E - Use DISCO power (when available)
• ↑ - BLAST (unlocked at level 5)
• S - BOOST (+25% speed, unlimited use)
• F - VIBES (unlocked at level 10, 1 per 10 levels)
• P / Tap Screen - Pause mission
• M - Toggle audio on/off
• TAB - Settings menu

DISCO DEGREE POWERS (Collect degree boxes):
• 👻 Stealth Mode - Temporary invisibility from criminal forces
• ⚪ Fair Play - Turn white, destroy criminal competitors on contact (+30 ETHER each, 6s)
• ⏰ Slow Time - Use technology to manipulate time
• 🔵 DISCO STRIKE - Fire cyan DISCO at criminal speeders (+20 ETHER)
• 💣 Degree Trap - Deploy mine trap for criminals (+10 ETHER)
• 🔄 Position Swap - Technology teleport with leading criminal
• ⚡ Speed Boost - Temporary speed enhancement

SCORING SYSTEM:
• Degree crystals (coins): +1 point each
• Item crates: Random DISCO power
• Red containers: Random vehicle skin or color change
• Fair Play mode criminal kill: +30 ETHER
• DISCO STRIKE hit: +20 ETHER
• VIBES kill: +15 ETHER (destroys drones, criminal riders, helicopters)
• Criminal hits your mine: +10 ETHER
• Defeat Helicopter: +500 ETHER
• Level progression: Every 5 ETHER = next level

HAZARDS TO AVOID:
• Criminal debris and degree mines
• Helicopter swarms (appear level 2+)
• Criminal vehicles (hostile riders)
• Obstacle spheres (reduce ETHER)
• Drone gangs (levels 2+)
• Criminal Strongholds (levels 10, 50, 90)

SPECIAL FEATURES:
• Dynamic city weather system (levels 5-25)
• Terrific Quest quotes floating in degree space
• DISCO acceleration jumps at maximum speed
• Fair Play degree glow when holding powers
• Bright red pulsating rider power (level 10+)
• Customizable vehicle skins, trails, and outfits
• Immersive 3D spatial audio (press M to enable)
• Real-time mission statistics

EXTREME DIFFICULTY: Level 60+ features enhanced criminal forces and faster gameplay!
